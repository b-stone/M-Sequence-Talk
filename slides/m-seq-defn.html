$M$-sequence definitions

Defnintions:

Given a multicomplex $\scr{M}$, define $m_i$ to be the number of monomials of degree $i$

The $M$-sequence is $$m=(m_0,m_1,m_2,\dots)$$

Example:

$$\scr{M}=\{{\color{red}{\underbrace{1}_{1}}}, {\color{orange}{\underbrace{x,y,z}_{3}}}, {\color{green}{\underbrace{x^2,xy,yz,z^2}_{4}}}, {\color{blue}{\underbrace{x^3}_{1}}}\} \rightarrow ({\color{red}{1}},{\color{orange}{3}},{\color{green}{4}},{\color{blue}{1}})$$
